Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

SwipeIt Auto Accepter ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Accessibility Service Disclosure (CRITICAL)

Our app uses the Android Accessibility Service API to provide automation features. This service is essential for the app to function as intended.

  • How we use it: We use the service to detect when an order popup appears in the "Porter Partner" application and to perform the "Swipe to Accept" gesture on your behalf.
  • No Data Collection: We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data using the Accessibility Service. All screen processing happens locally and in real-time on your device.
  • User Control: You can enable or disable this service at any time via your device's Accessibility settings.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Information: We collect your email address via Google Sign-In solely for account identification and subscription management.
  • Local Logs: We store activity logs (e.g., "Match Found", "Swipe Performed") locally on your device to help you monitor app activity. These logs are never uploaded.

3. Data Usage

We use your data only to:

  • Identify your subscription status.
  • Perform the automation tasks you have configured.
  • Improve app performance.

4. Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase for authentication and Google Play Billing for subscriptions. These services may collect data as governed by their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Security

We do not store your screen content or personal activity on any external servers. Your filter settings and logs stay on your device.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
